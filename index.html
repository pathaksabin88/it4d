Abstract

Agriculture is one of the potential sector for economic development and employment growth of Nepal. Nearly 68% of livelihood is depended on agriculture accounting for about 34% of the gross domestic product (GDP). Nevertheless, Nepal struggles to produce an adequate supply of food for its citizens. The population growth, affordability and economic improvement has further increased the demand of food production. Farmers have limited access to improved seeds, new technologies, and market opportunities. The total land used for cultivation has been decreasing every fiscal year. Rapid urbanization and housing in the land that was formerly used for cultivation have been seen as major causes for the decrease in the area of land used for cultivation. In such scenario it is vital to adapt technology to enhance the production of agriculture and food product. The Internet of Things could be one of the promising technology to improve productivity by monitoring and controlling in a right way using various sensors, actuators and cloud services.

The proposed IoT based monitoring devices goes through various phases. It first acquires data from different sensors and then it will analyze and process those data. The processed data and the result of analysis determines the act of actuators. The sensor data can also be visualized graphically.
The proposed IoT based monitoring device uses Arduino (a microcontroller), different sensors, a Raspberry Pi and sends the data to the cloud via WiFi module. The Arduino constantly measures three values: the soil pH value, temperature and humidity and soil moisture using the available sensors. The measured values are logged on to the Raspberry Pi on regular interval with the help of serial communication. The logged values are then regularly sent to the cloud from the Raspberry Pi, which is connected to the Internet via the WiFi module.
On the cloud end, the values are received, stored and visualized. The agriculture specialist and the concerned farmer have the ability to view and monitor the level of obtained soil ph value, temperature, humidity and moisture regularly. With an advanced implementation of the system using actuators and controller modules, it gives the ability to control different field devices so as to increase or decrease these values as needed.
